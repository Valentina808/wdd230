WDD 230: DESIGN PRINCIPLES

ALIGNMENT

Company name: Amazon
URL: Amazon
Amazon does a good job with alignment. They use a grid layout to show the products available. To the left is an image of the product that all have the same height. Next to each product is the product name which is top-aligned with the product image. If the product name wraps to multiple lines, then it is left aligned. Under the product name is the price which is left aligned with the product name.

REPETITION

Company name: Ikea
URL: Ikea
Repetition of yellow and blue colors in different parts of content reminds the color of the logo and makes the web page easily known. The way of showing name, parameters, and price is the same for every object (name -bold typeface, price -red color, bigger size, etc)

PROXIMITY

Company name: Facebook
URL:Facebook https://www.facebook.com/
The website's objects are grouped nicely and you can easily find what you need. For all needed icons grouped on top, it is a place for putting your thoughts, pictures, etc. to communicate, it is a group of stories and then all shared information.
